Guidelines for Paper Submission

Follow these formatting and submission guidelines carefully to ensure your paper is considered for publication in the proceedings.

Home›Submission Guidelines
Step by Step

Submission Requirements

01

Author names, affiliations, postal address, and e-mail should be typed below the title.

02

Pages for ISBN book chapters: 4–6 pages.

03

Paper on A4 size — 25 mm left margin, 20 mm on all other sides. Highlight aim, procedures, results & conclusions.

04

Graphical abstract is encouraged but not mandatory.

05

At least one author should register and present the work.

06

Full paper in .doc format must be emailed to icisse2026@gmail.com.

07

Paper abstract file must be named as "Presenting Author Name".

08

Entire paper in Single Column, Times New Roman, size 12.

Quality accepted papers (4-6 pages) published in ISBN book with DOI. Extended papers after peer review published in Scopus indexed journals. Mail full paper to icisse2026@gmail.com.

Invitation to Authors

Authors are invited to submit extended papers on Science, Technology, Engineering & Education. Present your work, receive expert feedback, and network for future research collaborations.
